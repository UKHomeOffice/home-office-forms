Apply to bring immediate family to the UK

The Afghan Citizens Resettlement Scheme (ACRS) provides a safe and legal way for some of the most vulnerable and at-risk people from Afghanistan to come to the United Kingdom and rebuild their lives.

Pathway 1 is for people evacuated or called forward under Operation PITTING, the UK military evacuation from Afghanistan.

Who can apply

Only people who have been invited can use this service. This is normally Pathway 1 Afghan nationals evacuated under Operation PITTING.

Who you can apply to bring

You can apply to bring immediate family members. You can also apply to bring additional family members in exceptional circumstances.

How the application works

Use this form to submit your application. The Home Office will then check if the family in your application are eligible.

You can save your progress and return at any time. We will store your application for 3 months after you start. After 3 months you will lose any progress.

! Warning You must sign in to your application before 23:59 on 27 August 2024. The form will close to new applications after this date.

We will sign you out if you don’t do anything for 1 hour. You will lose any unsaved progress.

Signing in to your application

To sign in to your application, you will need:

  • your date of birth (on your biometric residence permit card)
  • your biometric residence permit number or your unique application number
  • an email address you can access

What you will need

To complete your application, you will need:

  • personal details of family you are applying to bring
  • personal details of your family that live in the UK
  • your own personal details (name, date of birth, contact details)
  • evidence to support your application
