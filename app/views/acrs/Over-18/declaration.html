If found eligible, the family members in your referral will need to travel to the nearest accessible Visa Application Centre.

If children in your referral are found eligible, they will need to make this journey with an adult. Children must not travel without an adult chaperone.

I acknowledge that:

  • children under the age of 18 must not travel without an adult chaperone
  • I can provide the Home Office with details of the adult who will accompany them up until the point at which they board a flight to the UK. We will contact you for this information at a later date.
