Additional family members


Additional family members means anyone who is not:

  • your husband or wife (spouse), or unmarried partner
  • your own biological child
! Warning You can only refer additional family members to come to the UK in exceptional circumstances. You must have evidence to proe this.

An example of exceptional circumstances is family members who rely on you for a high level of medical care.
