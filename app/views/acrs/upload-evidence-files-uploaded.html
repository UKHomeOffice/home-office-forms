There is no requirement to add evidence, but adding it will support your application. You can add anything that you think is relevant.

What you can provide as evidence
Some examples of evidence might include:
  • Tazkira cards
  • passports
  • birth certificates
  • marriage certificates
  • adoption certificates
  • family photos
  • school reports
  • bank transfers between you and the family member
  • social media messages between you and the family member
  • medical records

We accept other types of evidence that are not in this list.

You must upload a translation for evidence that is not in English.
